About Oncospace.

A new approach to treatment planning.

Personalized Radiation Therapy

Current treatment planning attempts to standardize treatment for all patients. Oncospace creates a plan personalized to every patient, providing the optimal quality of care.

Improved Efficiency

Significantly reduce the amount of time it takes to create a top-quality plan. Our auto-planning will create a high-quality plan in under 5 minutes.

Ensured Security

Oncospace is a HIPAA compliant platform. Two-factor authentication and encrypted storage ensures top security for our clients and their data.

Knowledge Base.

A learning database accessible to all.

Our Knowledge Base is a database made up of previous treatment plans that describes the radiation dose given for different shapes and contours of tumors. It is backed by the Oncospace Consortium, a network of institutions that feeds specialized data into the platform.

  • Allows user to utilize whole Knowledge Base or specific segments
  • We work with leading institutions to gather the highest quality data
  • Gives every clinic the same plan quality as leading institutions.

Our Collaborators:

Optimization Engine.

An efficient engine to generate high-quality plans.

Our Optimization Engine does all the work. Fed a patient scenario, the Engine utilizes the Knowledge Base to compare the scenario to previous patients and the resulting plans. This solves for the optimal values of all the parameters in making a treatment plan. This reduces the amount of optimization runs in making a plan from 25-40 to 1-2 runs.

As our Knowledge Base grows, the more refind our Optimization Engine becomes, creating more optimal plans in smaller amounts of time.

Treatment Planning Tool.

A tool to create a better plan instantly.

The Treatment Planning Tool is an API used to access the Optimization Engine. It works with leading treatment planning systems so it is very easy to integrate into current clinics. We handle the infrastructure for the tool so that clinics only need to do their job.

The tool allows institutions to choose between different subsets of the knowledge base. Any user can choose to use the full power of the knowledge base, or just that of the leading institutions. Using our system, all radiation therapy clinics can offer the best possible treatment plans to patients.

Institution Backed.

Working with the best in the industry.

We work with the best radiotherapy clinics and hospitals worldwide to gather quality data. Our Knowledge Base is a collaborative system in which our institutional partners give their specialized data and benefit from others.
